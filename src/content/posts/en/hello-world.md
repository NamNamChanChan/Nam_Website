Welcome — this is my personal corner of the web.

I'm Nam Chan (aka Alfred Nam), CTO at [Wistkey](https://wistkey.com). By day I help businesses adopt AI: private on-prem LLM deployments, agentic workflow automation, and the engineering teams to ship them. Outside of that I build side projects, follow markets, and practise yoga.

## Why a personal site

Three reasons, honestly:

1. **A place to think out loud.** Writing forces clarity. Topics here will span **tech, IT, AI, business, finance and yoga** — the things I actually spend my time on.
2. **One link that owns my identity.** Social platforms come and go; this site collects [everywhere I exist online](/links) in one place I control.
3. **A home for side projects.** Things I build end up on the [projects page](/projects), with write-ups when they deserve one.

## How the bilingual thing works

I write in **English and Traditional Chinese**. Some posts exist in both languages — the **中 / EN** toggle in the header jumps between them. Most posts will live in just one language, and that's fine.

## Follow along

There's an [RSS feed](/rss.xml) (and a [separate one for Chinese posts](/zh/rss.xml)). Or just email me at [nam@wistkey.com](mailto:nam@wistkey.com) — I read everything.
